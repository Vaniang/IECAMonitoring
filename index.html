Material Quality Dimensions (MQD)

MQD1 — Clarity & Comprehension Message and content are clear and easy to understand.
MQD2 — Relevance & Utility Information is relevant and useful to the intended audience.
MQD3 — Visual Appeal & Aesthetics Design, graphics, layout, and typography are professional.
MQD4 — Accuracy & Trustworthiness Information, data, facts, and spellings are accurate and reliable.
MQD5 — Engagement The material captures attention and keeps the audience engaged.
MQD6 — Overall Effectiveness The material achieves its purpose and conveys the intended call to action.
